About Me

Welcome to my personal corner on the web! I'm Jared, a passionate backend engineer based in Austin, Texas. My journey in the tech world has been driven by a fascination with how systems work and the art of building robust, scalable applications.

Professional Background

With approximately 2 years of hands-on experience in backend development, I have had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects. I specialize in crafting efficient and maintainable systems that solve real-world problems, leveraging modern technologies and industry best practices.

My technical expertise includes:

  • Python: Developing backend services and automation scripts with simplicity and power.
  • Go: Building high-performance applications that can handle large-scale data processing and concurrency.
  • Ruby: Building elegant web applications with concise, maintainable code.
  • JavaScript: Bridging the gap between front-end interactions and backend logic.

Additionally, my proficiency in AWS allows me to design and implement cloud solutions that are both scalable and secure, ensuring that my projects can handle modern performance demands.
